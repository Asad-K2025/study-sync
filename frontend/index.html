Quick actions
Getting started
  • Create a group via + New Group.
  • Select a group to open its chat.
  • Send a message with Enter (or click the send button).
  • Create a meeting poll from Schedule Sync or ✦ Create Poll.
Allocate calendar
  • Paste your webcal:// iCal URL into Allocate Calendar and click Sync.
  • If the URL cannot be fetched, open the .ics link and paste the raw text instead.
  • Class events will auto-block conflicting poll slots.
Themes
  • Toggle Dark mode in Settings.
  • Pick a theme color; each theme adapts to light and dark modes.
Troubleshooting
  • Unauthorized: your session expired. Log in again.
  • Meeting poll shows Forbidden: you are not a member of that group.
  • Schedule not updating: reload the app and sync again.
